How It Works

1

Report Waste

Citizens submit waste reports with photos, location, and priority level directly from the app.

2

Assignment

Admins review reports and assign them to the most suitable waste collectors in that area.

3

Collection

Collectors are notified, accept jobs, and head to the locations to clean and manage waste.

4

Verification

Collectors upload completion proof with photos. Admins verify and mark reports as complete.
