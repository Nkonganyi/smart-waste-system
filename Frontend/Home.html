⚙️ Process

How GroundClean Works

From spotting a problem to a clean street — in four simple steps.

1

📸 Report

Citizen spots a waste issue and submits a report with a photo, location, and priority level.

2

📋 Assign

Admin reviews the report and assigns it to the best available waste collector in that area.

3

🚛 Collect

Collector is notified, accepts the job, follows the optimized route, and cleans the site.

4

✅ Verify

Collector uploads proof photo. Admin verifies and marks the report as completed.
